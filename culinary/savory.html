Zambezian Chicken


Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (cut in halves or quarters)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 lemons (juice)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare Marinade: Mix coconut milk, garlic, lemon juice, paprika, chili flakes, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate: Coat the chicken and let it marinate for at least 2 hours (preferably overnight).
  3. Grill: Cook over medium heat on a grill, basting with leftover marinade until golden and cooked through.
  4. Serve: Pair with steamed rice, cassava, or a fresh salad.

Matapa with shrimp


Ingredients

  • 2 cups cassava leaves (finely chopped or ground)
  • 1 lb shrimp (peeled and cleaned)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup peanuts (ground)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the Cassava Leaves: In a pot, heat oil and sauté onion and garlic. Add cassava leaves and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add Coconut and Peanuts: Stir in coconut milk and ground peanuts. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  3. Add Shrimp: Mix in shrimp, season with salt, and cook until shrimp are pink and tender (about 5–7 minutes).
  4. Serve: Enjoy with rice or xima (Mozambican cornmeal).

Beef stew

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef (cubed)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Sauté Aromatics: Heat oil in a pot. Sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Cook Beef: Add beef cubes, curry powder, salt, and pepper. Brown the meat.
  3. Simmer: Add tomatoes and coconut milk. Cover and simmer for 30–40 minutes until beef is tender.
  4. Serve: Pair with rice, xima, or bread.
