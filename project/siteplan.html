Site Plan

Site Name

Flavors of Mozambique
This name represents the culinary diversity that exists in Mozambique

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to showcase Mozambique's culinary diversity and share recipes that make Mozambican cuisine unique.

Scenarios

What recipe from Mozambique can I try today?
What are the most famous and appreciated recipes from Mozambique?
Where can I find more information about recipes from Mozambique?
